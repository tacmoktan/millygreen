Be the change you want to see in the world

Here at Milly Green we strive to create really useful products that not only consider and minimise environmental impact but also support sustainable initiatives and organisations that give back to our amazing planet through our partner 1% for the Planet.

100% recycled cotton

Did you know that an average t-shirt takes around 700 gallons of water to make! That's 16 oil drums full of water. When recycled contton is used, the quantity of water is reduced to just 10 gallons. Planet happy designs is proud to be part of this change with all of our textiles being made from 100% recycled cotton.

100% recycled paper

No new trees were harmed in the making of our planet happy stationery! We use 100% post consumer waste, printed using friendly soy inks and 75% less water in production than traditional virgin-tree fibre items. We maintainour vibrant colours and patterns as well as looking and feeling like a premium quality product.

eco bamboo fibre

All of our planet happy bamboo products are made from sustainable bamboo fibre. Bamboo is great durable material that quickly regenerates and is 100% bigdegradable. Making our travel mugs, trays and lunch boxes as sustainable and eco friendly as they can be.

cornstarch (pla)

By using plant based materials such as cornstarch instead of plastic, planet happy waterbottles remain bpa free, containing no harmful substances and allows our products to be 100% biodegradable whilst reducing our co2 emissions.

rpet

If you didn't know, RPET stands for Recycled Polythylene Terephthalate. In short rPET is created by recycling plastics that were previously used as packaging materials. Used to create our planet happy foldaway shopping bag it can be fully recycled and helps to reduce co2 emissions by 32 percent.

recycled leather

Recycled leather is a composite of shredded discarded leather collected from the manufacturing of clothes, shoes, boots, handbags furniture, etc. These shreds are then mixed together with a binding agent and produced into sheets of different thiknesses and then coated into any given color.
